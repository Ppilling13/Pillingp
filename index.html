Biography

Automation Engineer | Agritech Innovator | Construction & CNC Specialist

Driven by a passion for automation and process optimization, I am an Automation Engineering student at McMaster University with a strong foundation in PLC programming, AutoCAD, IoT integration, and problem-solving. My goal is to revolutionize the agritech industry by developing cost-effective and efficient automation solutions, particularly in grain drying and egg farming data management. Beyond my academic pursuits, I own and operate a small business specializing in construction renovation and CNC projects, where I apply my technical expertise to deliver high-quality, customized solutions. My hands-on experience in project management, customer relationship management, and sales has strengthened my ability to bridge the gap between technical innovation and practical application. I am currently working on an automation solution for egg farmers to streamline data logging and compliance, combining edge computing with cloud integration for seamless data management. Additionally, I am developing an industry-ready automatic grain dryer that aims to provide a more affordable alternative to existing market solutions. With a strong commitment to efficiency, innovation, and real-world problem-solving, I am eager to contribute to projects that enhance productivity in agriculture and beyond.
